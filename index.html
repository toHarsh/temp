US DIESEL, headquartered in Fort Worth, Texas, is a premier aftermarket supplier of parts, tools, and test equipment for a broad spectrum of diesel and gasoline fuel injection systems.

Header

We utilize our 40+ years of industry experience, knowledge, and relationships to provide the fuel injection repair and service industry with the highest quality aftermarket products at the best value.

Header

Integrity and customer satisfaction are what drive our business and if you are searching for diesel fuel injection system parts for pumps and injectors, tooling, or test equipment to help you do your job, we are here to help. Our goal is to be the “Best Value” supplier for the products we offer.


We also buy and sell used tooling, test equipment, and cleaning equipment. Or, if you are looking for parts to service your test equipment, we have those too.
